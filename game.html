How to Play

  1. Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  2. Press SPACE to jump and double-tap to double jump
  3. Collect power-ups to gain special abilities
  4. Avoid obstacles and defeat enemies to progress through levels
  5. Reach the end of each level to unlock new challenges and rewards
